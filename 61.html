[span_18](start_span)[span_19](start_span)[span_20](start_span)

Propeller Control and Operation
How is the propeller's blade pitch changed?
[span_35](start_span)[span_36](start_span)
Blade pitch is changed using an electronically controlled hydraulic pitch actuation system, which uses metered oil pressure[span_35](end_span)[span_36](end_span).
In the event of an oil pressure failure, what will happen to the propeller blades?
[span_37](start_span)[span_38](start_span)
The propeller blades are counter-weighted to move towards a coarse pitch in the event of an oil pressure failure[span_37](end_span)[span_38](end_span).
What is the Propeller Electronic Control (PEC), and what does it do?
[span_39](start_span)[span_40](start_span)
The Propeller Electronic Control (PEC) is a dual-channel, microprocessor-based controller[span_39](end_span)[span_40](end_span). [span_41](start_span)[span_42](start_span)It uses inputs from the aircraft, propeller control system sensors, and the engine control system to control propeller pitch and speed[span_41](end_span)[span_42](end_span).
What components are electronically connected to the PEC?
[span_43](start_span)[span_44](start_span)
The PEC is electronically connected to the condition lever, power lever (through FADEC), Magnetic Pick-up Unit (MPU), Beta Feedback Transducer (BFT), Pitch Control Unit (PCU), and the Full Authority Digital Electronic Control (FADEC)[span_43](end_span)[span_44](end_span).
What is the function of the Pitch Control Unit (PCU)?
[span_45](start_span)[span_46](start_span)[span_47](start_span)[span_48](start_span)
The PCU is a hydromechanical device commanded by the PEC[span_45](end_span)[span_46](end_span)[span_47](end_span)[span_48](end_span). [span_49](start_span)[span_50](start_span)[span_51](start_span)[span_52](start_span)It meters high-pressure engine oil to a servo valve and controls the oil flow to the fine or coarse pitch chambers of the propeller to move the blades in the desired direction[span_49](end_span)[span_50](end_span)[span_51](end_span)[span_52](end_span).
What is the purpose of the Propeller Overspeed Governor Unit?
[span_53](start_span)[span_54](start_span)[span_55](start_span)[span_56](start_span)[span_57](start_span)
The Propeller Overspeed Governor Unit is an independent mechanical system that protects the propeller from overspeeding[span_53](end_span)[span_54](end_span)[span_55](end_span)[span_56](end_span)[span_57](end_span).
What is the Auxiliary Feathering Pump Unit used for?
[span_58](start_span)[span_59](start_span)[span_60](start_span)[span_61](start_span)
The Auxiliary Feathering Pump Unit provides an independent means to feather the propeller if the primary feathering system fails[span_58](end_span)[span_59](end_span)[span_60](end_span)[span_61](end_span).
What are the three propeller feathering systems mentioned in the document?
[span_62](start_span)[span_63](start_span)[span_64](start_span)[span_65](start_span)[span_66](start_span)
The propeller feathering systems provide autofeathering, alternate feathering and unfeathering, and manual feathering[span_62](end_span)[span_63](end_span)[span_64](end_span)[span_65](end_span)[span_66](end_span).
What is the function of the Automatic Underspeed Protection Circuit (AUPC)?
[span_67](start_span)[span_68](start_span)
The AUPC is a circuit within the PEC that prevents a catastrophic event from an unsolicited coarse pitch demand due to a software or hardware failure[span_67](end_span)[span_68](end_span). [span_69](start_span)[span_70](start_span)It sends a "drive fine" signal to the PCU during a propeller underspeed event caused by a common mode software error, ensuring the total aircraft thrust does not fall below the thrust of a single engine[span_69](end_span)[span_70](end_span).

What is the Beta Mode?
[span_75](start_span)[span_76](start_span)
The Beta range is from a power lever position above FLIGHT IDLE to the full reverse (MAX REV) position[span_75](end_span)[span_76](end_span). [span_77](start_span)[span_78](start_span)[span_79](start_span)[span_80](start_span)In this mode, the blade angle is set by the power lever input[span_77](end_span)[span_78](end_span)[span_79](end_span)[span_80](end_span).

[span_87](start_span)[span_88](start_span)[span_89](start_span)

What is the function of the 'hard' and 'soft' flight fine stops?
[span_90](start_span)[span_91](start_span)

[span_126](start_span)[span_127](start_span)[span_128](start_span)
The feathering pump is provided to give a back-up feather function if the primary system is inoperative, to enable feathering when the gearbox rpm is too low, and to allow unfeathering on the ground for maintenance[span_126](end_span)[span_127](end_span)[span_128](end_span).
